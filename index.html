LLM-Powered Agent Workflow Assistant

Live

"Enabling agents to resolve tickets independently — even on unsupervised shifts."

An AI-assisted workflow system that guides support agents through structured ticket research and resolution using internal knowledge bases, ticketing platforms, and communication tools. Built to solve a specific problem: agents needed supervisor input before they could confidently resolve complex tickets, creating bottlenecks and inconsistent quality across shift patterns.

Impact
  • Used daily by a 20-agent support team
  • Eliminated supervisor dependency for routine and semi-complex ticket types
  • Maintained consistent resolution quality across all shifts, including unsupervised overnight shifts
  • Built entirely without engineering support — prompt engineering and LLM instruction architecture only

Real-Time Risk Detection Layer

Live

"Surfacing legal, safety, and privacy signals before they escalate."

A risk detection system embedded within the customer support workflow. Analyses incoming contacts in real time for signals indicating legal risk, mental health concerns, data privacy issues, and physical safety flags — then routes flagged contacts through defined escalation protocols. Tuned deliberately toward sensitivity: a missed escalation in a high-risk situation is a worse outcome than a false positive.

Impact
  • Systematic risk coverage across all contacts regardless of agent or shift
  • Removes reliance on individual agent judgment for escalation triggers
  • Consistent escalation behaviour across supervised and unsupervised shifts
  • Reduces legal and reputational exposure from missed high-risk signals
